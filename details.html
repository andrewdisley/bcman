What is BarCamp Manchester 3

BarCamp Manchester 3 is an open, free-to-attend, participatory 1-day unconference. Unconferences differ from normal conferences in that the schedule is created and filled by the attendees with everyone running a session or talk about whatever subject they have a passion for. From Cocktail Making to Programming for Beginners, from Arduino Hacking to Photography, BarCamps attract people from all sorts of backgrounds, each sharing their expertise and experiences.

When

Saturday 21st August 2010

Where

BarCamp Manchester 3 is being held at Manchester Digital Laboratory, 36-40 Edge Street, Manchester M4 1HN

MadLab is located just off Oxford Road - Google Map | Google Street View

Travel

MadLab is close to Manchester Victoria train station, the M.E.N. arena and the Arndale Centre. There is on-street parking nearby, as well as private car parks.

Tickets

Thanks to our sponsors and supporters, tickets are free. However, the venue is strictly limited in capacity.

There will be no different categories of tickets. We will be issuing all tickets at once, and be requiring that all attendees confirm their attendance 10 days before the event. You'll be sent an e-mail with an RSVP link to confirm you attendance. If you fail to RSVP your ticket may be reallocated to someone one the waiting list.
