Schedule

How does registration work?

The Registration Desk is open from 9:00am, check-in at the desk. There is no need to print off the confirmation e-mail, just remember your own name and you’ll be given a name badge.

How do the sessions work?

Unlike traditional conferences, the schedule for sessions at a BarCamp are decided by the participants. After the welcome talk, there’ll be a chance for attendees to fill out the Talk Board.

Sessions are 25 minutes long including Q&A and there will be a 5 minute change over period between sessions.
